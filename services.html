Extensions

Add the space your family needs with a properly built extension — designed around how you actually want to live.

What it covers

Whether you need an extra bedroom, a bigger kitchen or a whole new floor, we build single- and double-storey extensions from the groundwork up. Phil talks through what you want first, agrees a clear plan, and manages the build so you always know what's happening and when.

What's included

  • Single- & double-storey extensions
  • Garage & loft conversions
  • Conservatories, porches & garden rooms
  • Outbuildings & new builds

Why it matters locally

Around Connah's Quay and Flintshire, a well-planned extension is often a better move than the upheaval and cost of moving house. Done properly, it adds real, usable space and lasting value to your home.

Renovations

Modernise, repair and transform your home with renovation work built to last — managed from first idea to final finish.

What it covers

From refreshing a tired room to refurbishing a whole house, we take on renovations of every size — including kitchens, bathrooms, plastering, flooring, joinery and structural changes. We manage the trades and keep the site tidy so the job runs smoothly from start to finish.

What's included

  • Full home refurbishments
  • Kitchens, bathrooms & wet rooms
  • Plastering, flooring & joinery
  • Structural alterations, RSJs & knock-throughs

Why it matters locally

Many homes across North Wales are older properties that benefit from sympathetic, properly finished updates. As a local, owner-run firm you deal directly with Phil — clear advice, fair pricing and no surprises along the way.

Brickwork & Masonry

Solid, neat brickwork and masonry — from new walls to repairs that keep the weather out of your home.

What it covers

Bricklaying is at the heart of what we do. We take on everything from garden and retaining walls to structural brickwork, blockwork and stonework, plus repointing and chimney repairs to keep older walls sound and watertight.

What's included

  • Bricklaying, blockwork & stonework
  • Garden & retaining walls
  • Repointing & structural brickwork
  • Chimney repairs

Why it matters locally

North Wales weather is hard on brickwork and pointing. Staying on top of repairs — and getting new work built properly in the first place — protects your home from bigger, costlier problems later.

Groundworks

Get the job started on solid ground — foundations, drainage and concrete work done right from the very first dig.

What it covers

Every good build starts below ground. We handle site clearance, excavation, foundations and footings, drainage, concrete works and underpinning — the essential groundwork that everything else depends on.

What's included

  • Foundations, footings & underpinning
  • Site clearance & excavation
  • Drainage installation & drain repairs
  • Concrete slabs, kerbing & trenching

Why it matters locally

Groundwork is the part you never see but always rely on. Getting drainage and foundations right from the start saves expensive problems down the line — especially on the mix of older and sloping plots around North Wales.

Roofing

Keep the weather where it belongs with new roofs, repairs and watertight detailing.

What it covers

We fit and repair both pitched and flat roofs — tiling and slating, leadwork, fascias, soffits and guttering. From a few slipped tiles to a complete new roof, we'll tell you honestly what's needed and put it right.

What's included

  • New roofs, repairs & flat roofs
  • Roof tiling & slate roofing
  • Leadwork & chimney repairs
  • Fascias, soffits & guttering

Why it matters locally

With the wind and rain we get across the North Wales coast, a sound roof is your home's first line of defence. Catching small issues early keeps water out and avoids damage inside.

Driveways & Patios

Hard-wearing driveways and patios that look the part and stand up to North Wales weather.

What it covers

We lay block-paved, resin, tarmac and concrete driveways, plus patios and paths built on a properly prepared base so they stay level and drain well for years. Tell us how you'll use the space and we'll suggest the right finish.

What's included

  • Patios & paths
  • Block paving
  • Resin & tarmac driveways
  • Concrete driveways

Why it matters locally

A tidy driveway and patio lift the whole look of a property — and a properly laid base means no pooling water or sinking slabs after the first wet winter.

Landscaping

Make the most of your outdoor space with landscaping, decking, fencing and walls built to last.

What it covers

From reshaping a garden to laying a new lawn, we take on landscaping projects of all sizes — turfing and artificial grass, decking, fencing and garden walls, tied in neatly with any paving or groundwork.

What's included

  • Landscaping & garden walls
  • Turfing & artificial grass
  • Decking
  • Fencing

Why it matters locally

A well-finished garden is somewhere you'll actually use through the spring and summer. We build it to handle the local weather so it still looks good year after year.

Property Maintenance

Dependable repairs and upkeep that keep your home or rental in top condition — we turn up when we say we will.

What it covers

No job is too small. We handle general repairs, refurbishments, damp and leak repairs, and insurance, fire and storm damage work, plus interior and exterior decorating — with the same care and tidiness as a big project.

What's included

  • General repairs & refurbishments
  • Damp & leak repairs
  • Insurance, fire & storm damage work
  • Interior & exterior decorating

Why it matters locally

As a local firm we can get to you quickly when something needs sorting — and being owner-run means you get an honest assessment of what does (and doesn't) need doing.
