Our building services in North Wales

Home Renovations

Modernise, repair and extend your home with renovation work built to last — managed from first idea to final finish.

What it covers

From refreshing a tired room to refurbishing a whole house, we take on renovations of every size. Phil talks the job through with you first, agrees a clear plan, and then manages the work so you always know what's happening and when.

What's included

  • Kitchens, bathrooms & full refurbishments
  • Structural alterations & knock-throughs
  • Plastering, joinery & finishing
  • Tidy site and clear updates throughout

Why it matters locally

Many homes around Connah's Quay and Flintshire are older properties that benefit from sympathetic, properly finished updates. As a local, owner-run firm you deal directly with Phil — clear advice, fair pricing and no surprises along the way.

Property Maintenance

Dependable repairs and upkeep that keep your property in top condition — we turn up when we say we will.

What it covers

Whether it's a one-off repair or ongoing upkeep, we handle the everyday building jobs that keep a home or rental sound and looking after itself. No job is too small, and you get the same care and tidiness as a big project.

What's included

  • General repairs & small building jobs
  • Brickwork, pointing & rendering
  • Landlord & rental property upkeep
  • Honest assessment of what does (and doesn't) need doing

Why it matters locally

North Wales weather is hard on brickwork, pointing and rendering. Staying on top of small repairs protects your home from bigger, costlier problems later — and as a local firm we can get to you quickly when something needs sorting.

Garage Conversions

Turn an underused garage into a warm, dry room you'll actually live in — an office, playroom, utility or extra bedroom.

What it covers

We convert single and integral garages into properly finished living space — insulated, weatherproofed and plastered ready to decorate. You tell us how you want to use the room and we build it to suit.

What's included

  • Insulation, walls & weatherproofing
  • Flooring, windows & doors
  • Full conversions, plastered & ready to decorate
  • Finished to a standard you'd be happy to live in

Why it matters locally

A garage conversion is one of the most cost-effective ways to add usable space to a North Wales home without the upheaval of a full extension. Done properly, it gives you a comfortable room that's warm and dry all year round.

Not sure which service you need?

Tell us a little about your project and Phil will point you in the right direction — with a clear, no-obligation quote. Free advice, no pressure.
